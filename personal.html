Personal


Welcome to the personal section of my website! This portion of the website is dedicated to the various eclectic interests that I have outside of research.


Carnegie Hall

As a Carnegie Hall Music Ambassador, I give guided tours of Carnegie Hall during its regular season. Weekly tour schedules can be found here.

In addition, Carnegie Hall provides $10 student tickets to select performances, and $20 tickets to all Carnegie Hall presentations for individuals part of the Notables program. Anyone under the age of 40 can join the Notables program, and annual memberships start at only $20. If you like classical music, this is one of the best deals in town!
